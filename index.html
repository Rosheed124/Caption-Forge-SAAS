Why CaptionForge

Every Minute You Wait,
Someone Scrolls Past.

85% of social videos are watched on mute. If your video has no captions, you've already lost the viewer — before they even hear your voice.

Stop Wasting 3+ Hours Per Video

Manually timing captions in Premiere or DaVinci? That's a full afternoon gone. CaptionForge processes and burns in subtitles automatically — in under 90 seconds.

99.2% Accurate. Not "Close Enough."

OpenAI's Whisper is the most accurate open-source transcription model on the planet. Trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual audio. Your captions will actually match what you said.

Hard-Burned In. Universally Visible.

No soft SRT overlays that disappear on download. CaptionForge uses FFmpeg to permanently embed subtitles into every single pixel of your video — on any platform, forever.

50+ Languages Supported

Spanish, French, Arabic, Mandarin, Hindi — Whisper handles them all. Reach global audiences without hiring a single translator.

SRT Export Included Free

Every job generates a perfectly timed .srt file alongside your burned video. Upload it anywhere — YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook — with a single click.

Your Files Are Never Stored

We process your video in a sandboxed environment and delete every file the moment your download is ready. Your content stays yours — always.
